Whale Sightings Database

The Whale Sightings Database records reported sightings of whales and dolphins in Australian waters. The occurrence data has been displayed on the map below, come to find out which species have been found near your home! The different color along with the number on the map indicates the different density of the whale occurrence, the purple color means the highest density while the blue indicates the lowest. The prediction of whale occurrence date and location has been provided based on the analysis of the historical data, if you are going to plan your whale watching trip, you are in the right place!
